ABOUT US

From humble beginnings four students from the University of Technology, Jamaica took it upon themselves to start a company to educate the minds of the world through providing different services to the general public.

In 2014, Duran Brooks, Agyei Masters, Andre Pothemont and Richard McKenzie, had an idea to offer services to the world that were rarely offered to the level that they would provide.

In 2015, that dream was actualized and gave birth to the company Alacrity Professionals Ltd (APL). Through hard work and perseverance Alacrity Professionals Ltd (APL) has soared to the highest of heights offering the very best in the services outlined. Now everyone in anticipation is wondering what next from these four young entrepreneurs.
